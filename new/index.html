Snakesteroids

Snakesteroids is a mashup of two classic arcade genres. I combined the movement of asteroids and the rules of snake. The player controls a white spaceship using the arrow keys. They must gather white circles while avoiding pursuing red spaceships that perfectly follow the player's route. Initially made in 2 days for Mini Ludum Dare #67.

Overpopulate!

Overpopulate is a turn based strategy/board game. The idea is that both players must expand their territory to avoid overpopulating their land. Both players' choices are treated as happening at the same time. The goal is to outsurvive your opponent. This was my Mini Ludum Dare #69 submission made in 2 buggy sleepless days. There were several submissions at the time regarding the evils of colonization so I wanted to convey that perhaps colonization is caused by less evil intentions. Given perfect co-operation or just the fiercest aggression from both players, the game can easily end in a tie. In retrospect, I should probably have used a more understandable user interface. Perhaps give each player a set of cards to play that represent their moves and revealing the cards when both players are finished. If the embedded time doesn't work, skip to 6:38.
