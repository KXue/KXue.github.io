Musical Missile Mania

Summary: A "rhythm" game made in Unity for PTBO Game Jam 3 (Theme: retro flashback). In this Missile Command-like game, the player must play musical notes on a keyboard to fire missiles to intercept enemy missiles.

What I'm proud of: I repurposed a wireframe shader to look like retro neon lights. The effect was simple and effective for the given theme.

What needs work: I sacrificed gameplay for aesthetics, creating a significant delay between button press and missile launch. Additional feedback also showed that players were unfamiliar with the button layout meant to emulate a piano. I also created no incentive for playing real chords over just spamming notes.
