Pirates.net

Summary: A puzzle game inspired by Pipe Dream made for PTBO Game Jam 4 (Theme: Pirates without borders) made in Phaser with Matthwe Greer (@FlopshotQQ). The player rotates nodes (servers) to connect themselves with hidden cryptocurrency while avoiding connecting to the FBI.

What I'm proud of: That the game was engaging. My teammate and I worked well together and managed stay agile, constantly iterating on the MVP. Feedback in general has been positive.

What needs work: While difficult to solve at first, once the player realizes certain optimizations, all the difficulty seems to go away. This may be improved with better level design or the addition of new systems. Also, the game lacks conveyance of the goal and mechanics.
