Magiator

Summary: A networked PVP third person shooter made in Unity with a partner for class. Two players dodge and shoot fireballs at each other.

What I'm proud of: This was my first networked project. I had a lot of fun figuring out the networking problems such as syncing animations. I liked the UI as well.

What needs work: The game has no real win condition, and lacks many of the staples of a networked game (lobby, chat, game over screen, scores). In order to avoid scope creep and to deliver on time, I had to cut the game down to a MVP.
