Snakesteroids

Summary: A game jam project made for Mini Ludum Dare #67 (Theme: classic arcade mashup) made in Phaser. In this Snake and Asteroids mashup, the player collects rings which spawns clones that acts as the player's tail. Once the player touches their tail, the game is over.

What I'm proud of: As my first phaser game, I was proud I managed to make something that people found fun.

What needs work: The limitations of the engine and my knowledge of it at the time made the rest of the game unpolished. For example, The collision system used non-rotating squares rather than actual polygons.
