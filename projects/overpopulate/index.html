Overpopulate!

Summary: A "digital" board game made for Mini Ludum Dare #69 (Theme: colonization) in Phaser. In this game, turns resolve simultaneously. Players can discuss their moves with their opponents then move their population from tile to tile. Players must not look at the moves of other players. When a tile reaches population capacity, everyone in the tile dies. The goal therefore, is to make sure you outlast your opponent.

What I'm proud of: That this project managed to convey the push factors of colonization rather than focusing purely on the evils. This was also my first game to be featured on someone else's youtube video (link).

What needs work: This game is buggy and poorly coded. I had a lot of struggles with the hexagonal layout of the board.
